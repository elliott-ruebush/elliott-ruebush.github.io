For some pages, I have tags denoting at what point in the creative process they fall. There's not a commitment to move from one stage to another, but it's more just an FYI for the reader and a note for my own benefit if I'm looking to iterate on initial pages. 

The below are loose definitions of the three different tiers I might tag with.
### #raw
Could literally just be train-of-thought writing. Perhaps it's just a list of bullet points. It could have incomplete sentences and may not have a clear structure.
### #draft 
Written up into some sort of essay/review format. Perhaps trimmed down and with actual sentences/paragraphs
### #polished
Edited and refined to some degree. Whether or not it's "actually" polished is of course up to the reader.
