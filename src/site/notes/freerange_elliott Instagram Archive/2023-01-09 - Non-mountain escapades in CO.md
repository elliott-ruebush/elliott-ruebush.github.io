Non-mountain escapades from trip to Colorado. Really just an excuse to flex our $0.99 cake tbh

Pic 1: the absolute steal of the century. They misprinted a 0.99 sale sticker instead of 9.99 sale sticker, so we paid a dollar for a full mint chocolate cake. Calories per dollar were off the charts 📈 at ~6840 calories in the whole cake. This number comes in higher than the calories per dollar on things such as flour (4464 cpd), bread (3333 cpd), pure sugar (2854 cpd), rice (2320 cpd), oats (2148 cpd), and basically anything. The frosting did taste pretty strongly like toothpaste but hey who can complain for a dollar.

Pic 2: who says you can’t eat the exact same dinner 9 nights in a row. Pictured here is some of the fuel that kept us going. Kroger pasta sauce (actually a p solid jarred sauce), ground turkey, and spaghetti makes for an excellent meal 🍝
