Celebratory pow day post! ☃️ There is not in fact good snow all the time but here are some highlights from today and a few weeks back.

First up, we have #dropcornicesnotbombs
Super fun drop and hoping to come back and full send huck this one bigger and probably crash but hopefully look cool doing it if we get good conditions for it again.

Next, we’ve got some beautiful fresh snow after a 30ish minute bootpack (hike) up to Flute bowl.

Third is a pic with some of the other guys on the course I’m doing.

Fourth, we’ve got me about to do a quick hike up to an area called grey zone that’s not quite out of bounds but also isn’t exactly an official run. Hiking to places usually = better snow because it requires extra effort. Plus you get some fun bonus fitness! 

Then we’ve got a bunch of pretty snowy mountain pics (may not always coincide with good snow conditions).

Overall super stoked after today and hopin for another few big dumps before I finish up in Whistler in another couple weeks ❄️
