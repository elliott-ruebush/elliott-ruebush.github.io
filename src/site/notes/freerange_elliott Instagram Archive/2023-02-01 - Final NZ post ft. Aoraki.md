Last location of our time in New Zealand! Spent a few days hiking around Aoraki/Mt Cook National Park. Aoraki/Mt Cook is the highest peak in New Zealand and simply looms over everything around it. It’s like a shot straight out of a movie or something and once you see it you KNOW that’s the biggest mountain around. 

There’s also a ton of other rly cool glaciated peaks around Aoraki/Mt Cook that you can see throughout these pics. 

Other content that can be seen includes me right before taking a quick dip in an iceberg-filled glacial lake 🏊‍♂️🥶 , my attempts at some sunrise photography 🌄, me doing a cool trick™️, me lovingly staring up at the additional 3000ft of vert we have to hike 🥾⬆️ , and me pointing at the cool glaciers with a rainbow behind me 🌈🧊
