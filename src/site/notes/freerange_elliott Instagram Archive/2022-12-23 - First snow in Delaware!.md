In honor of the first snow of the year in Delaware (action packed video included), here’s a throwback to one of my first big trips snowboarding. 

My high school had a ski club that organized a trip or two per year up to the northeast. In 2019, we took a bus to Sunday River in Maine and I officially fell in love with snowboarding as a more serious pursuit.

I always can say I wish I started sooner, but I’m happy to be where I am now with the opportunity to travel to snowboard in all sorts of cool places.
