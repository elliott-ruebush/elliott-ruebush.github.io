An absolutely beautiful 8 days of ripping it up at Vail in Colorado. While the company Vail Resorts may be questionable, Vail the ski resort has some cool terrain.

I may have developed a minor calf strain on the last day, John’s feet may have ended up with too many blisters to count, and Jackson may have gotten a sweet sled ride down with ski patrol, but that doesn’t take too much away from the amazing days of skiing we had. (Disclaimer: no one suffered too severe of injuries we’re all gonna be alright)

We got fresh snow on almost half our days of skiing, meaning that both the on and off-piste runs were a blast. Lots of time was spent practicing drops, popping off sidehits, and flying through the trees. 

Swipe to view me steal johns ski, hug a tree (I didn’t hit this tree but I did hit two [small] trees at the same time another day), and for some of Jackson’s follow cam content.
