Successfully made it to New Zealand after a long journey and have begun the January summer road trip. 

So far we’ve spent most of our time vibin at the beach in Hahei and gettin absolutely toasted by the summer sun bc hemispheres switch seasons and winter me was not ready for it.

New Zealand is cool bc it’s like temperate rainforest vibes and the plants and stuff are like slightly different than back home.

Also, we did some cave rafting and saw bioluminescent glow worms which were actually super cool and a totally unique experience. 

Overall it’s been a very cool summer vacation interjected into winter so far and I’m looking forward to the next things we’ll be doing.
