Some sights n views from my time in Japan so far. Just 2 days left here before heading off to Seoul for the weekend!

Definitely has been a change of pace from snowboarding in Canada but has been a really cool experience overall.

Pics start and end with Mt Fuji because it’s a pretty fricken rad stratovolcano. It’s the highest peak in Japan and is extremely prominent, rising high above any surrounding mountains.

Besides that, we’ve got a great collection of shrines, parks, and city views. 

1/10 - Fuji in clear conditions midday

2/10 - Senso-ji shrine in Tokyo

3/10 - Fushimi Inari shrine Tori gates in Kyoto

4/10 - Preserved ruins of a building that was destroyed in the blast from the atomic bomb detonated over Hiroshima in WWII. Hiroshima was definitely worth a visit and it’s sobering to think about the damage one bomb did.

5/10 - garden in Hiroshima

6/10 - street in Asakusa in Tokyo

7/10 - view of part of Tokyo from 45 stories up (this city is absolutely massive)

8/10 - down in the trees in the park shown in the previous image, was cool because there’s like old growth trees in middle of city.

9/10 - video from Shinjuku Gyoen Garden in Tokyo 

10/10 - sunset Fuji over Lake Ashi
