Utah gets a solid rating of👍👍

In May 2021, we got a good group together, rented a car, and went national park touring. We hit Canyonlands, Arches, Bryce Canyon, and Zion and got hikes in at each place. 

The desert regions of Utah have a ton of fascinating rock formations and there’s an endless supply of scenic hikes to be had at each of these national parks. 

Shoutout to the town of Moab for being an sick place to stay as well. Tons of interesting people and a primo location right next to Canyonlands and Arches as well as all sorts of other outdoor opportunities.
