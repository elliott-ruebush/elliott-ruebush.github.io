Some nice shots from going out touring last weekend + some bonus content of me not sticking the landings from a week or two back. Video in slide number 6 will not disappoint.

Conditions are currently a bit tough due to lots of melt-freeze cycles and a lack of a fresh snow. But it’s still always a good time out on the mountain and we’ve had some beautiful blue sky days. I’ve been doing lots of touring this week with my AST2. Besides that, I also skied a classic big mountain line on Blackcomb with some of my group yesterday which was pretty cool. Avalanche hazard is low with the stable weather and lack of fresh snow, so it’s a great time to take on some more complex terrain. 

Will post an update once I finish up this week, especially if I get some cool pics to share.
